*Strategy Analyst — Major U.S. Metro Area*

Vantage Strategy Group is a management consulting firm that works with Fortune 500 clients on market entry, competitive positioning, and corporate transformation. Founded in 2011, the firm has grown to over 400 professionals across six offices.

**The Role**

As a Strategy Analyst, you will develop competitive landscape analyses, build financial models to evaluate strategic options, and prepare client-facing presentations that translate complex data into actionable recommendations. You will work directly with engagement managers to scope research questions, conduct primary and secondary research, and synthesize findings for senior leadership at client organizations.

**Who We Are**

We are redefining how strategic advisory works. Our team operates at the intersection of rigorous analysis and bold thinking — we do not recycle frameworks, we build new ones. We recruit people who want to be part of building something that has not been done before, who find energy in problems that do not have obvious answers. Our culture rewards intellectual courage, moves fast on high-conviction ideas, and holds every deliverable to a standard that reshapes how our clients think about their markets.

**Requirements**

Bachelor's degree in business, economics, or a related field. Two to three years of experience in consulting, corporate strategy, or a related analytical role.
