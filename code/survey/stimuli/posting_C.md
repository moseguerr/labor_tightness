**Crestline Industries**
*Marketing Coordinator — Major U.S. Metro Area*

Crestline Industries is a mid-size consumer products company with a portfolio of brands in home goods and personal care. Headquartered in the greater Washington, D.C. area, the company distributes through major retail partners and a growing direct-to-consumer channel.

**The Role**

As a Marketing Coordinator, you will manage campaign timelines and budgets across digital and traditional channels, coordinate with external agencies on creative assets, and analyze campaign performance data to optimize spending. You will support the marketing director in developing quarterly plans, preparing competitive analyses, and tracking brand health metrics across key customer segments.

**Working at Crestline**

We offer a structured mentorship program that pairs every new hire with a senior leader, a clear promotion track reviewed annually, and flexible hybrid work arrangements with three days in-office. Employees receive a dedicated professional development budget of $2,000 per year for courses, conferences, or certifications of their choosing. We have been recognized as a top workplace for professional development by the Washington Business Journal for three consecutive years. Our benefits include comprehensive health coverage, a 401(k) match, and four weeks of paid time off starting in your first year.

**Requirements**

Bachelor's degree in marketing, communications, business, or a related field. Two to three years of experience in marketing, brand management, or a related coordination role.
