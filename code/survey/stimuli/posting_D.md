Novalink Group is a workforce technology firm that builds predictive analytics platforms for enterprise human resources teams. The company serves over 150 mid-market and enterprise clients and was recently named to a list of fastest-growing HR technology companies.

**The Role**

As a People Analytics Analyst, you will design and maintain dashboards that track workforce metrics such as attrition risk, engagement drivers, and hiring funnel conversion. You will run statistical analyses on employee survey data, build predictive models for talent retention, and present findings to client HR leadership teams to inform their people strategy decisions.

**Who We Are**

We are building the future of how organizations understand their workforce. Our team sits at the frontier of applied behavioral science and machine learning — we believe the next generation of people decisions will be driven by evidence, not intuition. We recruit people who are energized by hard problems and want to pioneer approaches that do not exist yet. Our culture is built on rigorous thinking, fast iteration, and a shared conviction that we are creating something that will fundamentally change how companies invest in their people.

**Requirements**

Bachelor's degree in data science, industrial-organizational psychology, business analytics, or a related field. Two to three years of experience in analytics, human resources research, or a related quantitative role.
