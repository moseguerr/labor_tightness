# Card Sort Screen — Study 2, Stage 2

Presented after wages are revealed on all five postings. One card sort per posting, presented in random order across participants.

---

## Transition Screen (shown before card sort begins)

> We can now reveal the salary ranges for each position. Take a moment to review this information before continuing.

*[Display all five postings with salary ranges visible. Enforce a minimum 30-second wait before the "Continue" button activates.]*

---

## Card Sort Instruction (shown for each posting)

> Imagine you are preparing a short cover letter opening for this position. From the list below, select the **three statements** that best describe what you would want to emphasize in your application to **[COMPANY NAME]**.

*[Display the full posting text with salary visible above the card pool.]*

---

## Card Pool

12 cards total. Participant selects exactly 3 per posting. Cards are displayed in randomized order for each posting.

### Identity Cards (I) — responding to purpose signals

| ID | Card Text |
|----|-----------|
| I1 | My passion for this organization's mission and what it is working to achieve |
| I2 | My alignment with this company's values and the kind of work they stand for |
| I3 | My belief in what this organization is building and my desire to be part of it |
| I4 | My fit with how this team thinks and operates |

### Good-Employer Responsiveness Cards (G) — responding to employer investment signals

| ID | Card Text |
|----|-----------|
| G1 | My long-term commitment to growing within an organization that invests in its people |
| G2 | My appreciation for environments that support professional development and work-life balance |
| G3 | My interest in the specific development opportunities and resources this firm offers |

### Competence Cards (C) — pure productivity signal, employer-type-neutral

| ID | Card Text |
|----|-----------|
| C1 | My technical skills and analytical background relevant to this role |
| C2 | My track record of delivering results in similar positions |
| C3 | My academic achievements and relevant coursework |
| C4 | My leadership experience and ability to work across functions |
| C5 | My ability to contribute immediately given my prior experience |

---

## Implementation Notes

- Enforce exactly 3 selections per posting. Show a counter: "Selected: 2 of 3."
- Randomize card display order independently for each posting.
- Present postings in a random order that differs across participants.
- Record which 3 cards were selected, the order of selection, and the timestamp for each posting.
- The full posting (with salary) should remain visible while the participant makes their selections — do not require them to recall from memory.
- Card labels (I1, G1, C1, etc.) are NOT shown to participants. They are for researcher reference only.

---

## Predicted Card Profiles

| Posting | Expected dominant card type | Rationale |
|---------|----------------------------|-----------|
| A (Purpose-Innovation + High) | Identity (I) | Purpose language + high wage = credible identity signal |
| B (Purpose-Social + Low) | Mixed I/C shift | Purpose language undermined by low wage; shift toward competence |
| C (Good-Employer + High) | Good-employer (G) | Explicit development/mentorship language + high wage |
| D (Purpose-Innovation + Low) | Mixed I/C shift | Same as B — purpose undermined by low wage |
| E (Neutral + Medium) | Competence (C) | No employer signal → default to productivity signaling |
