Informed Consent

You are being invited to participate in a research study conducted by Mariana Oseguera at Georgetown University's McDonough School of Business.

Purpose

This study investigates how people interpret and respond to language used in job postings. Your responses will help advance research on how employers communicate with job seekers.

What You Will Do

You will complete one or two short tasks on the computer. The tasks involve reading phrases or job postings and providing your judgments. Everything is on-screen and self-paced. The study takes approximately 12-19 minutes depending on the session.

Confidentiality

All responses are anonymous and will only be reported in aggregate. No individual participant's data will be identifiable in any publications or presentations resulting from this research. No names or identifying information are collected.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty by closing your browser or clicking the "Withdraw" link.

Contact

If you have questions about this study, please contact the Principal Investigator, Professor Mariana Oseguera.
