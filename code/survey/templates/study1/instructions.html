Instructions

In this task, you will see phrases that come from real job postings in the United States.

For each phrase, please check ALL categories you think it fits into. Some phrases may fit more than one category — that is completely fine. If a phrase does not seem to fit any category, select "None of these / I'm not sure."

There are no trick questions. We want your honest judgment about what each phrase is communicating to a job seeker.

You will start with a few practice items to get familiar with the categories. After the practice round, you will begin the main task.

Tip: A reference panel with category definitions will stay visible on the side of your screen throughout the task. You can refer to it at any time.
