Category Reference

1. The company's mission, values, or social/environmental impact

What the company stands for, its broader purpose, or efforts to make a positive difference.

2. The company treats employees well

Workplace culture, career development, flexibility, recognition, supportive management.

3. Pay, benefits, or financial perks

Salary, bonuses, insurance, retirement, stock options, PTO, tuition reimbursement.

4. Job tasks, duties, or responsibilities

What someone in this job would actually do day-to-day.

5. Job requirements or qualifications

Degrees, experience, skills, certifications needed.

6. None of these / I'm not sure

Doesn't fit any category, or genuinely unclear.
