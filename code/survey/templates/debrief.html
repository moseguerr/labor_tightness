About This Study

We are studying different types of employer branding in job postings and how they are interpreted by hiring managers and job seekers. The job postings you evaluated were fictional, constructed based on common characteristics of real ones, specifically for this study. We systematically varied aspects of the postings — including whether the company emphasized a social mission, innovation, or being a great workplace, as well as the salary offered.

Confidentiality

Your responses are completely anonymous and will only be reported in aggregate. No individual participant will be identifiable in any publications or presentations.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this study, please contact the Principal Investigator, Professor Mariana Oseguera.
