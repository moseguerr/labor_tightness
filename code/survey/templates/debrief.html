About This Study

The job postings you evaluated were fictional, created specifically for this study. We systematically varied aspects of the postings — including whether the company emphasized a social mission, innovation, or being a great workplace, as well as the salary offered.

{% if study_assignment == 'study2' %}

We are studying how these features — particularly claims about organizational purpose and mission — influence job seekers' evaluations. This research investigates whether organizational purpose claims function as genuine signals of employer quality or as substitutes for concrete benefits like salary.

{% elif study_assignment == 'study3' %}

We are studying how hiring managers perceive and evaluate different types of employer branding in job postings. This research investigates how organizational purpose claims compare to other framing strategies when attracting talent.
